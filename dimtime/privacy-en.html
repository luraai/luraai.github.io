Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026 · 한국어

DimeTime (Korean title: 급여계산기, "the App"), published by LuraAI, respects your privacy and handles your information as described below.

1. Data We Collect — None

The App does not collect any personal data.

  • The operator runs no server that stores or can read your data.
  • The App bundles no third-party analytics or marketing SDK, and does not record or analyze how you use the App.
  • The App does not gather device identifiers or link your data with other companies, apps, or websites.
  • The App contains no advertising and uses no advertising identifiers or tracking technologies.

2. What the App Stores

The information below is entered by you and is stored only on your device and your own iCloud. The operator cannot access it, so under Apple's definition this is not "collection."

  • Workplace details (name, payroll rule settings)
  • Staff names, hourly wages, work schedules, and notes
  • Clock-in/out records (start/end times, breaks)
  • Employment-contract copies and profile photos (only if you add them)
  • App settings (payday, tax rate, week start day, etc.)

3. Device Permissions (Camera · Photos)

The App requests device permissions solely for the purposes below, and only uses them with your consent.

  • Camera: to photograph an employment contract or a staff member's profile photo.
  • Add to Photos: to save a copy of an employment contract to your photo library.

Captured or saved images stay on your device and your own iCloud, exactly as described in Section 2, and are not transmitted externally.

4. iCloud Sync

If your iCloud account is active, the App's data is stored in a CloudKit Private Database that belongs to your own Apple account and syncs across your devices. The operator cannot access this data, and Apple's privacy policy governs that storage.

The App works fine without iCloud — local storage only.

5. Sharing With Third Parties — None

The operator does not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties. There is no data in the operator's possession to begin with, and the App integrates no third-party SDKs.

6. In-App Purchase (Unlock Pro)

Unlock Pro is a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase (₩9,900) processed through Apple's App Store (StoreKit). Apple handles the payment entirely; the operator does not collect or store your payment method or card details. The App only checks whether the purchase exists in order to raise your usage limits.

7. Data Deletion

  • You can delete workplaces, staff members, or work records directly within the App.
  • To delete all data, remove the App via iOS Settings → the App → Delete App, which also removes data from iCloud.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you through an App update and this page.
