Frequently asked

Do I need an account?

No. kWord has no accounts. Install it and start studying.

Will I lose my progress on a new device?

If iCloud sync is on and you use the same Apple Account, your progress follows you. If you do not use sync, go to Settings → Export data, then import the file on the new device.

How do I cancel my subscription?

In iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions, pick kWord and cancel. Cancellation goes through Apple; we cannot do it for you.

I want a refund.

Refunds for App Store charges are handled by Apple. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

My purchase is missing.

Tap Settings → Restore purchases in the app. If it still does not appear, check that you are signed in with the Apple Account you bought it with, then get in touch.

Pronunciation scoring does not work.

It recognises speech on the device only. Check that kWord has Microphone and Speech Recognition permission in iOS Settings. On a device that cannot recognise speech locally, the feature turns itself off.

I found a mistake in a word or sentence.

Tell us and we will fix it. Use Settings → Send feedback in the app, or email us with the word in question.

Can other people see what I have been studying?

No. Only if you opt in to the league does an auto-generated anonymous name and your weekly XP appear on a shared leaderboard. Your words, mistakes and recordings are never published. See the privacy policy for the detail.
