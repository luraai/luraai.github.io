Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026 · 한국어

kWord has no accounts and sends nothing to our servers. The app contains no code that talks to a LuraAI server, and no analytics, tracking or advertising SDK of any kind. Your learning data lives on your device, and — if you turn sync on — in your own iCloud.

1. What we collect

LuraAI (“we”) collects no personal information through kWord (the “app”).

  • There is no sign-up and no account.
  • We never ask for or collect a name, email, phone number or location.
  • We use no analytics, tracking or advertising frameworks, and ship no third-party SDKs.
  • The app contains no networking code that sends data to us.

2. What is stored on your device

Everything the app produces stays on your device:

  • Per-word progress, review scheduling, correct and incorrect answers
  • Study sessions, XP, level, streak, quests and achievements
  • App settings (language, theme, daily limits, reminder times)
  • App-usage events, which power the statistics screen and help diagnose problems. They never leave the device unless you explicitly export them to a file yourself.

3. iCloud sync — your storage, not ours

If you use sync across devices, your learning data is stored via Apple’s CloudKit in your own iCloud private database. That storage belongs to your Apple Account, and we cannot access it. Sync follows your device’s iCloud settings and can be turned off in iOS Settings at any time.

4. League — opt-in, and public

This section applies only if you turn the league on. The default is off.

If you opt in to the weekly league, the following is published to a public CloudKit database visible to other players:

  • An auto-generated anonymous display name. The app invents it; you do not type it, and it is not derived from your name or email.
  • Your weekly XP and level — the numbers the ranking is built from.
  • An anonymous token — a random UUID held in your device’s Keychain. It is not tied to your Apple Account or to any identifying information.

The “friend code” used by the friends feature is derived from that same token and carries no identifying information. Your words, mistakes and pronunciation recordings are never published. Turning the league off stops the publishing.

5. Device permissions

The app may ask for the permissions below. Declining any of them leaves the core learning features fully working.

PermissionWhy
Microphone To hear you during pronunciation practice. Recordings are neither stored nor transmitted.
Speech Recognition To score your pronunciation. Recognition runs on-device only (requiresOnDeviceRecognition); on a device that cannot do it locally, the feature simply turns itself off.
Notifications To remind you when reviews are due. Every reminder is scheduled locally on the device — no push server is involved.

6. In-app purchases

Subscriptions and content packs are sold through Apple’s StoreKit. Payment and receipt validation are handled entirely by Apple; we never receive or store your payment details or Apple Account information. Entitlements are checked on your device.

7. Sharing and selling

We collect no personal information, so there is none to share with or sell to anyone.

8. Cookies and tracking

The app uses no cookies, web beacons, advertising identifiers (IDFA) or other cross-app tracking technology.

9. Children

We collect no personal information from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+.

10. Deleting your data

Deleting the app deletes the learning data on your device. Anything synced to iCloud can be managed and removed in iOS Settings → your Apple Account → iCloud. Turning the league off stops any further publishing. The app also offers export, import and a full reset.

11. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “last updated” date above changes with it, and material changes are announced in the app.

12. Contact

Questions about privacy: contact@luraai.io
