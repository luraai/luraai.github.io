Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

Overview

AutoAlignPanels ("the App") is developed and published by LuraAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

AutoAlignPanels does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information.

Specifically, the App:

  • Does not collect personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
  • Does not collect usage analytics or telemetry
  • Does not use tracking technologies or advertising identifiers
  • Does not access the internet or connect to any servers
  • Does not use cookies

Accessibility Permission

The App requires macOS Accessibility permission to move and resize windows. This permission is handled entirely by the macOS operating system. The App uses this permission solely to perform window management operations on your local device. No window content, titles, or other information is collected or transmitted.

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (keyboard shortcut, window gap size, menu bar visibility) locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
